Shihao Ji

Email:   shihaoji@gmail.com

I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University in 2006. My advisor was Dr. Lawrence Carin. I also worked closely with Dr. Ron Parr and Dr. Layne Watson.

Research Interests

  • Deep learning (word embedding, neural language models, machine translation, CNN/RNN/LSTM, large-scale machine learning)
  • Machine learning (Probabilistic graphical models, Bayesian inference, compressive sensing, web search ranking)
  • Decision-making under uncertainty (POMDP, reinforcement learning)
  • Parallel computing, high performance computing, software optimization
